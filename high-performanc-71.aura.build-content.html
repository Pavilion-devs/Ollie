Our Process

We make home ownership possible. Step by step.

MI is designed to work alongside the existing housing and financial system - not around it. Each step is transparent, compliant, and built for long-term ownership.

01 / ASSESS

Understand Your Buying Power

We start with your situation - income, savings, and the homes you’re considering. This defines what’s possible today, and where shared equity can help.

02 / CO-INVEST

Buy Together

MI co-invests in the home with you at purchase. You become the 100% homeowner. We share in the equity - so you can buy your dream home.

03 / GROW

As the home grows in value, so does your ownership. You can buy out MI over time, or settle when you sell. Simple, aligned, and long-term by design.
